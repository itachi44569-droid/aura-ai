FAQ

Common questions.

Do I need to pay for any APIs?+
No. Every API is completely free. Groq (AI + Whisper), Pollinations (images), DuckDuckGo (search), Open-Meteo (weather) — all free with no credit card required. Railway's free tier handles hosting.
What AI models are included? Can I switch?+
Yes — there's a live model switcher in the UI. Four tiers: Fast (Llama 3.1 8B — instant), Smart (Llama 3.3 70B — strong reasoning), Deep (DeepSeek R1 — best for complex tasks), and Gemini Flash. All free. The model shows as a badge on every response.
What's the knowledge base for?+
It lets Aura answer questions about your own documents. Paste text or give it a URL — it stores the content and retrieves relevant chunks when users ask. Perfect for customer support bots, internal wikis, or specialized assistants trained on your content.
Can I use my own domain name?+
Yes. Railway supports custom domains for free. After purchasing, point any domain you own to your Railway deployment. The deploy guide includes step-by-step instructions — takes under 5 minutes.
Can I resell or charge users for access?+
Yes. After purchase the code is yours. Rebrand it, charge users, sell it as a SaaS, or use it as a client project. The only restriction is you cannot resell the source code itself as a template.
What do I need to deploy this myself?+
Basic comfort with a terminal and a free Railway account. The deploy guide walks through every step — no Python or FastAPI experience needed. If you'd rather have it done for you, Tier 1 covers that completely.
